Morning Office Routine

⇩ Download audio/01-morning-office-routine.mp3

IntroHello everyone, and welcome back. Today, we are going to learn some simple English to describe a morning office routine. Let's get started!

Arriving at the OfficeEvery morning, I arrive at my company. I walk through the main door and say "Good morning" to the receptionist. After that, I scan my fingerprint to check in. This records my arrival time. Then, I press the button and wait for the elevator to go up to my office.

Breakfast with a ViewSometimes, I arrive a little late. When this happens, I don't go straight to my desk. Instead, I go up to the top floor to have breakfast. The cafeteria is a beautiful space. It has a great view of the city and the river. It is very relaxing before a busy day.

Setting Up the WorkspaceAfter breakfast, I walk to my desk. Now, it is time to set up my workspace. First, I open my laptop. Then, I plug in my external monitor for a bigger screen. I also connect the power cable, my wireless mouse, and my keyboard.

Getting Ready to WorkFinally, before I start working, I take out my notebook. I write down three things I need to do today. I organize these tasks from high priority to low priority. This simple habit helps me stay focused all day.

OutroThat is my morning office routine! What about you? What do you do when you first arrive at your office? Thank you for listening, and see you in the next episode!

The Water Cup on My Desk

⇩ Download audio/03-water-cup-on-my-desk.mp3

IntroWelcome back. Today, let's talk about something very important on my desk: my water cup.

The Cup on the DeskThere is always a big cup on my desk. I put it right next to my laptop so I can see it easily. It is a very simple thing, but I use it every single day.

When and How to Use ItI use this cup to drink warm water or tea. I drink when I feel thirsty, of course. But I also take a sip of water when I need a short break. Looking at the computer screen for too long makes me tired, so drinking water helps me feel fresh again.

How Much Water to DrinkPeople say we should drink about two liters of water a day. That is about eight glasses. Because I spend most of my day at the office, I try to drink at least four or five full cups of water here. It keeps me healthy and gives me energy to work.

OutroDrinking enough water is a simple but great habit. How much water do you drink at work? Thanks for listening, and see you next time!

Office Lunch Break and Nap Time

⇩ Download audio/04-office-lunch-break-and-nap-time.mp3

IntroHello everyone, Welcome back. Today, let's explore what we usually do during our office lunch break.

Going Out for LunchAt lunchtime, we have many food choices. Usually, we eat rice because it makes us full and helps us save money. But sometimes, we want to try different dishes. The restaurants are a bit far from our office, so we have to drive or ride our motorbikes to get there.

Bringing Lunch from HomeSome of my coworkers choose not to go out. They bring their own lunch boxes from home. This is a great way to save money, eat healthy food, and make sure the food is clean. Plus, they have a lot more time to rest.

Coffee or Napping?After eating, we sometimes go to a cafe to chat and relax. However, most of the time, we spend our remaining break time taking a nap.

The Importance of Taking a NapTaking a nap is very important to me. Just a short sleep of 15 to 30 minutes helps us recharge our energy and focus on our work in the afternoon. We even have personal lockers at the company. I keep my sleeping items, like a small pillow and blanket, in my locker. This helps me have a very good and deep sleep.

OutroThat is my lunch routine! Do you prefer going out for lunch or bringing your own food? Thanks for listening, and catch you in the next episode!

Weekly Team Meeting

⇩ Download audio/05-weekly-team-meeting.mp3

IntroHi everyone, Welcome back. Today, we are going to talk about a very common office activity: the weekly team meeting. Let's begin!

Booking the Meeting RoomEvery week, my team has a regular meeting. Because our company does not have many meeting rooms, I always have to prepare early. I use the company system to find and book a room at least two days in advance. If I forget, we won't have a place to meet!

Checking Project StatusDuring the meeting, the first thing we do is talk about our projects. We share what we finished this week. We check if the work is on time or late. If someone has a problem, everyone listens and tries to help.

Planning for Next WeekAfter checking the projects, we make a plan for the next week. We look at our goals and I assign new tasks to my team members. We want to make sure everyone knows exactly what to do.

Other Information and NewsFinally, we spend time for other information. Sometimes, I share new company rules or updates from the management. Other times, we just talk about fun things, like planning a team dinner or celebrating a member's birthday. It is a great time to connect with each other.

OutroMeetings help my team work better together. How often do you have meetings at your work? Thanks for listening, and see you in the next episode!
